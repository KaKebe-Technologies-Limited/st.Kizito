School Objectives

To instil discipline in the learners that will guide them even after school.

To provide quality academic foundation for the child’s future

To provide mentorship among the staff and pupils to enhance good leadership skills

To shape learners into useful and confident individual of integrity

School Curriculum

St. Kizito Primary School, Lira offers Uganda national primary school curriculum. Pupils who are at the end of their primary school year (P.7) will be assessed using Primary Leaving Examination by Uganda National Examination Board.

What kind of pupils does SKIPS produce

The pupils of St. Kizito Primary School, Lira will develop into God fearing, caring, innovative and creative thinkers who are accommodative to one another. The pupil will be nurtured to be morally upright, open minded and knowledgeable to overcome the challenging future.

Our Teachers

Our teachers are well trained and experienced to nurture pupils in all aspects of life. They guide and work with the children in all school activities to ensure that children acquire the right knowledge, skills, values, independent work ethics and interpersonal skills. Our teachers undergo professional development training to provide exposure and understanding the curriculum expectations and contemporary issues. Our staff are carefully selected and recruited from different parts of the country to enable sharing experiences on a broad spectrum.

Management

The Bishop of Lira Diocese is responsible for the administration of the school together with the Diocesan Education Commission and the School Management Committee (SMC). The day to day running of the school is managed by the head teacher. The school is also staffed with well trained and experienced staff from all parts of the country.
